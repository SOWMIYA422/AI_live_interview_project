1. Technical Score (1-10)
   Basis: Measures the technical accuracy and depth of the answer

Scoring Criteria:

9-10: Expert-level technical knowledge, precise terminology, demonstrates deep understanding with specific examples

7-8: Strong technical grasp, correct concepts, good use of terminology, some practical examples

5-6: Basic technical understanding, some inaccuracies, limited terminology, vague examples

3-4: Significant technical gaps, major inaccuracies, poor terminology use

1-2: Completely incorrect or irrelevant technical information

Factors Considered:

Accuracy of technical facts and concepts

Relevance to the specific job role requirements

Depth of technical knowledge demonstrated

Use of appropriate industry terminology

Practical examples and real-world applications

Problem-solving approach and methodology

2. Communication Score (1-10)
   Basis: Evaluates how effectively the candidate communicates their answer

Scoring Criteria:

9-10: Exceptionally clear, well-structured, concise, professional tone, excellent articulation

7-8: Clear and organized, good flow, appropriate tone, minor areas for improvement

5-6: Somewhat clear but disorganized, rambling, inconsistent tone

3-4: Unclear, poorly structured, unprofessional language, difficult to follow

1-2: Incoherent, completely unstructured, inappropriate communication style

Factors Considered:

Clarity and organization of thoughts

Conciseness and focus on key points

Professional language and tone

Confidence and articulation in delivery

Logical flow and structure

Ability to explain complex concepts simply

3. Overall Score (1-10)
   Basis: Comprehensive assessment combining technical and communication aspects

Scoring Criteria:

9-10: Outstanding answer that exceeds expectations in all areas

7-8: Strong answer demonstrating competence and good preparation

5-6: Adequate answer meeting basic requirements but lacking depth

3-4: Weak answer with significant shortcomings

1-2: Poor answer failing to address the question properly

Factors Considered:

Overall quality and completeness of response

Evidence and examples provided

Problem-solving and critical thinking demonstrated

Relevance to the job role and company context

Professionalism and interview etiquette

Ability to handle follow-up potential
